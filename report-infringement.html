Report Infringement

Intellectual property and brand misuse — we take reports seriously.

If you believe Gigahive content or listings infringe your rights, or if you see misuse of the Gigahive brand, please report it with as much detail as possible.

What to include

  • Your name and contact email.
  • Description of the intellectual property or issue.
  • URLs or screenshots of the page in question.
  • Statement that you believe the use is in good faith unauthorised.

Submit by email

Send the above to legal@gigahive.in with the subject line “Infringement report”. We review submissions and respond when action is taken.
