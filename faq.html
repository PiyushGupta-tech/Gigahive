Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about orders, payments, sizing, and delivery.

How do I place an order?

Add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your address, and pay with cards, UPI, net banking, or COD where available.

How do I track my order?

After dispatch, you will receive a tracking link by SMS or email. You can also check your order status in your account if you are logged in.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, UPI, net banking, and COD on eligible orders. See our Payments page for details.

How do I choose the right size?

Refer to the size chart on each product page. When in doubt, measure a favourite garment and compare with our measurements.

How can I contact Gigahive?

Visit Contact Us for our registered address and mail correspondence.
