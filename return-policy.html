Return Policy

Clear rules for returns, exchanges, and refunds — read before you shop.

This policy explains how returns work for products purchased on Gigahive. It applies in addition to your statutory rights.

Eligibility

Items must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags intact. Footwear and accessories should be returned in original boxes where provided.

Time window

Returns must be initiated within the period shown on your order or product page (typically 7–15 days from delivery) unless otherwise stated.

How to return

Use the “Return” option in your order flow or contact support with your order ID. We will arrange pickup or share a return address where applicable.

Refunds

After inspection, refunds are issued to the original payment method. COD refunds may be processed via bank transfer or store credit as communicated at the time.

Non-returnable items

Items marked final sale, innerwear, or hygiene-sensitive products may not be returnable unless defective.
